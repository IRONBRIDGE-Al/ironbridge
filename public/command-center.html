Gate System
🟢
GREEN — Execute Immediately
Agent acts without asking. Reports to Dick after. Examples: writing a file, health check, polling inbox, drafting copy for review.
🟡
YELLOW — Present to Dick First
Agent prepares, presents to Dick, waits for go-ahead. Examples: deploying code, publishing anything public, write-API calls, new integrations, spending money.
🔴
RED — Owner Approval Only
Nothing moves until Owner says yes in Telegram or repo commit. Examples: pricing changes, token launch, WhatsApp Meta key, ClawRouter, anything irreversible.
👁
Second Eyes — Every Significant Decision
Claude stamps completed tasks. Brooks logs health. Dick confirms leads. Owner is final on RED. Nothing is "done" without a timestamp + witness in command center.

❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a token launch?

A token launch is creating a new cryptocurrency on a blockchain. With IronForge, you deploy with one click - no coding required.

Do I need technical skills?

No! That's the point. Connect wallet, choose package, launch. Everything is pre-built and ready.

Is my money safe?

Yes. Your keys, your crypto. We use read-only wallet connections and self-custody. No private keys stored.

What chains can I launch on?

Base, Ethereum, Solana, Polygon, Avalanche - all included in every package.

How does $BNKR work?

Every trade of your token earns you $BNKR. It's built into the platform - automatic rewards.
